Max Temperature

As expected from one's expectations the temperature rises significantly as one approaches the equator.

The data does seem to indicate that the temperature is colder approaching the south pole as opposed to the north. This might simply be a result of the data being collected at random and having more locations selected at southern latitudes. However, when comparing temperatures at the same absolute latitude magnitude it does indeed seem that the southern hemisphere is colder. For example at -40 degrees latitude the temperature is approximately 40F, whilst at +40 degrees latitude the temperature roughly 60F - 70F.
